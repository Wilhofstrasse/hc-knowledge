What's outside this flow (deliberately)

Comment on §What's outside this flow (deliberately)
  • People photos / employee headshots — never auto-fetched. Privacy + GDPR + ethics. Manual upload only.
  • Real-time data feeds — sources are snapshotted at deck open, not live. Avoids "deck looked different yesterday" confusion.
  • Cross-tenant content sharing — explicit re-keying ceremony required (M&A scenario).
  • Send-side detection — Office.js can't intercept Save As / AirDrop / email-attach. Disclaimer footer is rendered into the deck as a fallback.
